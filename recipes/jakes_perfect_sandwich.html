A Perfect Sandwich

A sandwich matched by no other, this dish is known as the best sandwich in The land of Ooo.

After completion be catious of potential magic men who may try to steal it.

Ingredients

  • bread roll
  • rosemary
  • thyme
  • steak
  • pickles from your boy Prismo
  • creamcheese
  • diced boiled eggs
  • bird from the window
  • common cucumber
  • sliced roman tomato
  • sweet yellow onion...organic
  • tears for salt
  • bacon
  • lobster soul

Steps

  1. ready yourself to receive instruction from the realm of creation above you to concieve this sandwich
  2. cue violin music
  3. wash your vegetables
  4. sharpen knife exactly 6 times
  5. place thyme rosemary and steak in ziplock bag
  6. remove air from bag and sous vide steak at 135°
  7. slice and toast bread roll
  8. apply creamcheese to bread
  9. add pickles from your boy Prismo
  10. and some dill
  11. capture and cook a bird from your window
  12. add diced boiled eggs
  13. add bird from the window
  14. add cucumber,tomato,then onions
  15. add tears
  16. grill bacon and add to sandwich
  17. boil a lobster and capture its soul making sure to add it to sandwich
